Welcome to Icelandair Travel Info!

At Icelandair, we strive to make your travel experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Here are some important travel information and guidelines to help you plan your trip.

Booking a Flight:
You can book your flight with Icelandair through our website or mobile app. Our online booking system is user-friendly and secure, and you can easily search for flights, select your preferred seat, and pay for your ticket online. You can also contact our customer service team for assistance with booking or if you have any questions.

Baggage Allowance:
Icelandair allows passengers to bring one carry-on bag and one personal item on board. The carry-on bag must not exceed 22 x 14 x 9 inches (56 x 36 x 23 cm) and the personal item must fit under the seat in front of you. Checked baggage fees may apply depending on your fare class, route, and destination. Please check our website for the latest baggage allowance and fees.

Check-in and Boarding:
We recommend that you arrive at the airport at least 2 hours before your scheduled departure time for domestic flights and 3 hours for international flights. You can check-in for your flight online or at the airport using our self-service kiosks or check-in counters. Boarding usually begins 30 minutes before the departure time, and the boarding gate closes 15 minutes before the departure time.

In-Flight Services:
Icelandair offers in-flight entertainment, complimentary snacks and beverages, and a selection of meals for purchase on most flights. You can also bring your own food and drinks on board, as long as they comply with our carry-on regulations. We strive to make your flight as comfortable as possible, so please let us know if you have any special requests or needs.

Flight Status and Updates:
You can check your flight status and updates on our website or mobile app. We also provide real-time flight status notifications via email, text message, or push notifications. If your flight is delayed, canceled, or rescheduled, we will do our best to notify you as soon as possible and assist you with alternative arrangements.

Thank you for choosing Icelandair for your travel needs. We look forward to welcoming you on board!
